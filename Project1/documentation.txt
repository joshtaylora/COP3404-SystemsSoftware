NAMING CONVENTIONS
- typedef and structs use upper camel case 
    - ex: SymbolTable, Symbol
- methods involving the creation, management, and searching of a SymbolTable object have 'ST_' 
    before a verb describing what the method does in upper camel case (if more than 1 word)
    - ex: ST_Get, ST_Alloc
- local variables use lower camel case
    - ex: locCounter, lineNumber, address
